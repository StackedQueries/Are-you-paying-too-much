Privacy Policy

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for analytics (e.g. Google Analytics). We also use a client-side anonymous identifier stored in your browser to help us avoid duplicate form submissions.

Data you enter into the benchmark tool (age, premium, household size) is processed only in your browser and is not sent to our servers unless you explicitly submit the consent form.

If you submit the consent form, we collect your first name, email address, phone number (optional), and the data you entered into the benchmark tool. This information is stored in a private Google Sheet and used only to contact you about your coverage options.

Calendly. After you complete the benchmark, we offer an optional scheduling widget powered by Calendly (calendly.com). If you use it to book a call, Calendly will collect the information you provide (e.g. name, email, and any details you enter). That data is processed by Calendly according to their Privacy Notice. We use the resulting bookings to contact you about your coverage options.

We do not sell your personal information. For questions, contact the site owner.
