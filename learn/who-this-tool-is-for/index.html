Who This Tool Is For

Built for independent operators who want clarity around coverage structure.

This tool is built for self-employed business owners, independent contractors and 1099 earners, founders without employer-sponsored coverage, and anyone reviewing individual health plans.

If you operate independently and want clarity around your coverage structure — and whether your premium is in line with typical benchmarks — this tool is for you.

It is not a quote or underwriting. It is a fast way to see if your premium may be misaligned with typical structures. You'll provide basic contact and household details in the flow so we can show your benchmark and, with your consent, follow up with next steps.

Use the benchmark tool to get started, then explore our guides to understand different plan types and what they actually cover.
